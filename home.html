About Dr. Philip Hayes

Dr. Philip Hayes, an award-winning plastic surgeon, specialises in reconstructive and cosmetic surgery. With a Bachelor of Medicine and Surgery (MBBCh) cum laude, Dr. Hayes has honed his skills in prestigious institutions. He has presented and won numerous awards for clinical research papers, showcasing his dedication to innovation and patient care. Dr. Hayes is currently based at the Busamend Hillcrest Private Hospital.

International Fellowships

Dr. Hayes has enhanced his knowledge and skills through international fellowships. His first fellowship at the Royal Free Hospital in London focused on skin malignancies, breast reconstructive surgery, hand surgery, and cosmetic surgery. His second fellowship, conducted at the Wellington Hospital, was dedicated to cosmetic surgery. experiences have allowed him to collaborate with some of the world's leading plastic surgeons.
